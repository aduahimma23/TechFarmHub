About Techfarm

TECHFARM Hub is a Pan-African entrepreneurship, social innovation and business development hub promoting disruptive knowledge, skills, technologies and resources to support young entrepreneurs, innovators and startups, SMEs to upscale opportunities with productivity for profitability with specific focus on boosting smart agriculture and agribusinesses to promote sustainable development, Agenda 2063 and Africa Free Continental Trade Agreement (AfCFTA) in Ghana and Africa.

TECHFARM Hub was established in January, 2020 and officially registered with the Registrar Generals Department in January 2022.

Brief History

TECHFARM Hub emerged out of the over a decade operations of FAPIMPA Foundation a Civil Society Organisation passionate about leadership empowerment, charity works and skills training to improve lives, livelihoods and communities in Ghana, Africa and diaspora. FAPIMPA Foundation’s vision is; “Building Sustainable HOPE for Humanity’s Development”.

WH. Kobina Adomadzi Longdon the Developer and Chief Executive with apt support from Sylvia Safakor Senu an Economic Analyst andpassionate youth advocate as the Co-Developer established TECHFARM Hub in January, 2020 as the first Entrepreneurship, Innovation and Business Development Hub in the Eastern Region of Ghana to provide a safe, resourceful and empowering space for young Agripreneurs, Innovators and Startups to flourish disruptive ideas, upscale various innovative solutions to boost smart agriculture and agribusinesses and accelerate growth into productive and profitable ventures.

Albeit the initial startup challenges as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic and mobilization of resources, TECHFARM Hub continues to engage key ecosystem stakeholders to ensure the Hub consolidates ongoing tailored activities supporting target groups and individuals with relevant skills, resources and opportunities.
